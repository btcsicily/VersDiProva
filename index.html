MY EVO OIL

SICILIAN ORGANIC EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL

My Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oil from healthy, untreated olives has a fragrant and fragrant flavor and is an ideal companion for the best dishes of Mediterranean cuisine.
The olive groves cultivated in the lands of the Sicilian Coast, precisely in the territory between the Oriented Nature Reserve “Torre Salsa" and the famous Scala dei Turchi, with bilogical method, represent native varieties of western Sicily such as Biancolilla and Nocellara del Belice.

INFO

Superior category olive oil obtained directly from olives and solely by mechanical means.
Cold extracted.
Store in a cool dry place away from sources heat and directlight.
